About me

My name is Daiana and I'm 23 years old. I was born in Resita on 13 oct. 1999. I live in Timisoara, Romania. A quote by Marva Collins states, “success does not come to you, you go to it.” This is exactly how I look at my success. My definition of success is being happy and doing what you love. The only way I can achieve this goal is by receiving an education, saving money, and picking a career path that I will enjoy.

I've graduated "Liceul Teoretic Tata Oancea, Bocsa in 2018". I've studied mathematics and informatics in highschool and in my free time I attended a few IT courses for juniors (Java and C++). I've got "Ajutor de programator" certification in informatics and a few marketing certifications. After years of trying to find my career, I finally decided to apply to IT School. My goal is to become a successful Full-Stack developer and I'm happy to learn from the best.
